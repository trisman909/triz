# Lanternfall identity

The sky-lantern that anchored a drowned kingdom is dying. Each run, a Bearer
carries one surviving flame through ruins that rearrange around remembered
choices.

## Original pillars

1. **Light is battlefield geometry.** A movable radiance field changes enemy
   behaviors and attacks. Fighting beyond it is dangerous but more rewarding.
2. **Rewards form memories.** Relics called Echoes occupy a three-part chain;
   neighbors harmonize, clash, or awaken a third effect. Position matters.
3. **Routes are promises.** Branches offer Vows with visible conditions.
   Breaking one alters later encounters instead of merely deleting a reward.

Permanent progression primarily expands decisions. Difficulty adds behaviors
and relationships before health. Every enemy needs a unique silhouette,
telegraph, encounter role, and meaningful radiance interaction.

## Representative biome art direction

The five review chambers establish spatial identity before full-game
integration:

- **Drowned Narthex** is a narrow, processional T-shaped nave. Flood channels,
  barnacled arches, hanging bells and a raised reliquary pull the eye through
  cold cyan haze.
- **Siltglass Observatory** is a radial arena organized around a broken orrery.
  Curved routes, glass fins and half-buried instruments create circular
  traversal and a sandy-violet horizon.
- **Ember Ossuary** is a split furnace trench. Parallel elevated walks, a hot
  central hazard lane, bone-fed kilns and a distant furnace make movement feel
  directional and pressured.
- **Gloam Orchard** is an irregular root clearing. Offset islands, knotted
  trunks, memorial growths and asymmetric gaps deny a clean architectural
  axis and support organic circling.
- **Stormvault Foundry** is a broad machine grid. Conductive lanes, heavy
  pylons, turbine forms and a framed dynamo create deliberate, industrial
  traversal with long readable sightlines.

These scenes are approval prototypes for production language, not permission
to stamp one modular kit across the whole run. Full integration must preserve
each biome's surface family, silhouette grammar, material response, prop
vocabulary, traversal feel and landmark hierarchy.
